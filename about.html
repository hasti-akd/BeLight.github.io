Hi there, and welcome to BeLight!

Here at BeLight, our mission is to create a healthier, happier and more sustainable society and to challenge the stigma around mental health.

Background

The initial idea that led to the creation of BeLight originated from people who were working on a class project and were interested in and concerned about mental health and wellbeing.
They all believed in the importance of promoting mental health and created this website, which is growing daily.

Our Mission

It is important to us to provide informative, inspiring, and topical stories about mental health and wellbeing. We strongly believe that raising awareness of mental health conditions is the most powerful method of counteracting the shame many people struggling with these issues may feel.
